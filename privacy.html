🍺 PintPal · Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

PintPal is designed with your privacy in mind. This policy explains what data we handle and how we handle it.

1. Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. PintPal does not require an account, login, or any form of personal identification.

All data generated by your use of the app—including drink timestamps, session history, venue names, and preferences (such as dark mode)—is stored locally on your device using browser storage (localStorage). This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to any server or third party.

2. How We Use Your Data

Since all data remains on your device, we do not access, view, collect, or process your session data in any way. The data exists solely for your personal tracking and insights within the app.

3. Derivative Data and Analytics

PintPal calculates statistics and insights (such as average drinks per session, drinking pace, total pints, and streaks) based on your session data. All calculations are performed locally on your device using JavaScript. This derivative data is never transmitted to any server or third party. We do not have access to any of your session data, raw or derived.

PintPal does not use any third-party analytics services (such as Google Analytics, Firebase, or Mixpanel). We have zero visibility into how you use the app.

4. Third-Party Services

PintPal integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Font Awesome & jsDelivr CDNs: We load icons and fonts from these trusted content delivery networks to improve performance. These services may collect anonymized usage data for their own analytics.
  • Buy Me a Coffee: The "Buy me a pint" button links to Buy Me a Coffee, an external tipping platform. When you click this link, you are subject to Buy Me a Coffee's privacy policy. We encourage you to review it before making any payment. View Buy Me a Coffee Privacy Policy.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

PintPal does not use cookies, web beacons, tracking pixels, or any similar tracking technologies. The app uses your browser's localStorage API to save your session data and preferences locally on your device. This data is never transmitted to any server and is not accessible to us.

PintPal does not integrate with Google Maps, location services, or any geolocation APIs.

6. Google API Services

PintPal does not use any Google API Services. The app does not integrate with Google Maps, Google Sign-In, Firebase, YouTube, Google Drive, Google Analytics, or any other Google APIs that would require API keys or OAuth credentials.

7. Offline Functionality

PintPal uses a service worker to cache essential app assets (HTML, CSS, JavaScript). This enables the app to function offline. No personal or session data is stored in this cache.

8. Data Security

PintPal takes a minimalist approach to data security: we protect your privacy by not collecting your data in the first place.

All information you enter into PintPal remains stored locally on your device using standard browser storage APIs. This data never leaves your device and is never accessible to us or any third party.

The PintPal application code is delivered to your browser over a secure HTTPS connection. Beyond this, the security of your local data is dependent on the built-in security features of your device and browser.

Because we do not operate any servers or databases containing user information, there is no central data store that could be subject to a data breach. Your data is as secure as your device.

9. Children's Privacy

PintPal is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

10. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information. Since PintPal does not collect or store any personal information, there is no data for us to provide, correct, or delete. All your data is under your complete control on your own device.

You can delete all your PintPal data at any time by:

  • Using the "Clear all" button in the Past sessions section
  • Clearing your browser data for this website
  • Uninstalling the app

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
